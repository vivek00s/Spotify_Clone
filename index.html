How do I create a playlist? ⬇️⬆️

Playlists are a great way to save collections of music, either for your own listening or to share.


To create one:

  1. Tap Your Library.
  2. Tap CREATE.
  3. Give your playlist a name.
  4. Start adding songs (and we'll help you along).
